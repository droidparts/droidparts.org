DroidParts Support Library¶

Named after Android Support Library, droidparts-support.jar provides Fragments support on pre-3.0 Androids.

Overview¶

The base Activities & Fragments are grouped into the following packages:

  • org.droidparts.*.support.v4 - pure Fragments by Android Support Library.
  • org.droidparts.*.support.v7 - AppCompat implementation in Android Support Library.

Setup¶

  • Add android-support* projects to your app.
  • Add droidparts-support.jar to the libs folder & extend appropriate base classes.
